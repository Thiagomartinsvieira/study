MakerHero: the newest Arduino reseller in Brazil

MakerHero is the newest Arduino reseller in Brazil, starting to sell several models of boards from the electronic prototyping platform of Italian origin with exclusivity. Founded in 2010, the electronic components store is increasingly establishing itself as a promoter of the maker culture in the country.
